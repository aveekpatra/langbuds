STEP 1

You set your language in our app. The LangBuds will try to translate everything into this language.

STEP 2

Whenever someone within an one-meter radius of you tries to speak to you in a foreign language, the LangBuds will detect the speech and the language of the speech.

STEP 3

Then the LangBuds will translate the speech using Deepl translation API. After the translation process it will turn the text into a computer-generated speech.

STEP 4

It will play the computer-generated speech in your ear.

FINISH

As a result of this process, you can understand languages that you can't understand otherwise.
